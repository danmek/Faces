Background

This project is looking at a comparative analysis of the performance of two neural network architectures: a convolutional neural network (CNN) and a transformers network. The idea is to understand how they each do face processing, and which has a more holistic interpretation of faces, compared to human vision.

Hypothesis: the Face Transformers network is better at holistic face processing than FaceNet, a CNN
